# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want Lun to be a welcoming space for everyone, regardless of experience
level, background, or identity. The whole point of this project is that
different perspectives make better outcomes — that applies to people, not just
AI agents.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive. Critique ideas, not people.
- Assume good faith. Ask questions before assuming bad intent.
- Welcome newcomers and help them get oriented.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Personal or political attacks.
- Publishing others' private information without permission.
- Any conduct that would reasonably be considered inappropriate in a
  professional setting.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions, comments, and issues that
violate this Code of Conduct, and may temporarily or permanently ban any
contributor for behaviors they deem inappropriate.

To report an issue, contact the maintainer via the
[GitHub profile](https://github.com/soonsoon2).

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the
[Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org), version 2.1.
